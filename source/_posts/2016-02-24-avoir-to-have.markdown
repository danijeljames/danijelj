Like Ãªtre, avoir is an irregular verb.

| avoir | to have |
|:---|:---|
| j'ai (=je ai} | I have |
| tu as | you have |
| il/elle a | he/she has |
| nous avons | we have _(n)_ |
| vous avez | you have _(n)_ |
| ils ont | they have _m/f)_ |

Il a un voitre. > He has a bus.  
J'ai une montre. > I have a watch.
